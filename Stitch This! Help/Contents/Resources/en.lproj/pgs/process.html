Step-by-Step

The 'workflow' (i.e. the steps required, and sequence) is listed below, and follows the layout of the tabs in the app's main window.



The Next > button at the top right of each tab pane is enabled once you have done enough on that tab to progress to the next step. You can either click on the next tab in the tab set, or click the Next > button to proceed to the next step of the process.

Image

Open an image file and reduce it - both in size and number of colours

Match

Match the readied image to a thread-range and specific no. of colours

Symbols

Review and optionally change the symbols allocated by the app

Generate

Create printable output for your project
